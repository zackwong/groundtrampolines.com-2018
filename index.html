About HXD

Good Brothers Sports

Good Brothers Sports is the only manufacture producing in ground trampoline in China. Since established in 2004, the family founder committed to producing whole world's safest and most interesting playground trampoline for kids. Through company's continuous investment in research and technological innovation, with management of ISO9001:2000, we are proud to bring the third-generation buried playground trampoline to kids around the world. This revolutionary product brings kids more fun experience while ensuring their absolute safety. Different shapes and colors bring this product ability to blend in with the landscape, whether it is a playground or a backyard garden, it can be easily and efficiently installed.
