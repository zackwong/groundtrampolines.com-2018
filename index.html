Flexwarm

Flexwarm Heating Technology

Flexwarm's factory is located in Shenzhen, covering an area of 3000 square meters and owning self-developed auto production line which can fulfil 30K sets per day with only 2 technicists.

Since its establishment, Flexwarm has been focusing on the R&D, as well as production and applications of the thick film technology. It not only provides innovative heating products and sensors with high precision and sensitivity but also solutions of highly precise control in various harsh environments such as high temperature and high corrosion. Until today, Flexwarm has registered more than 200 global patents.
