--- layout: pages permalink: /factory/ category: about title: Flexwarm Factory ---

Leading by a senior engineer associated with Tiangong-1 in 2011, a group of hardworking engineers sweated on applying the military technologies, thick film heating technology and higher precision sensor technology, to products for civilian use.

It took them 5 years to develop the technology until the first generation of Flexwarm products were simultaneously released in China and overseas markets in 2016.

Comparing with existing electroheat products on the market, Flexwarm products offer a higher level of safety, energy efficiency, and comfortability, and thus, they enabled Flexwarm to get multi-million cloud funding on JD.com and Kickstarter.
